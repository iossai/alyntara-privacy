Alyntara — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Alyntara ("the app") is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data we collect

None. Alyntara does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no user accounts. If you choose to enable daily reminders, the app requests notification permission. Reminders are scheduled and delivered locally on your device, and the permission can be disabled at any time.

Data stored on your device

Your intentions, journal entries, and favorite affirmations are stored locally on your device only. They never leave your device. Uninstalling the app deletes this data.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Health disclaimer

Alyntara offers wellbeing practices such as breathing exercises and journaling. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Changes
